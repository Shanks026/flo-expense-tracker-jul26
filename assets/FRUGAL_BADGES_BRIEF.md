# Trophy Badges — Frugal

Match the style of the existing badge art already in this app (medal shape,
ribbon, illustrated icon) — same family, your creative call on the rest.

## Frugal — 3 tiers (a ladder)

Rewards no-spend days — days where nothing was spent at all. One badge per
tier, escalating in richness like the other ladders:

- 5 no-spend days
- 25 no-spend days
- 100 no-spend days (top tier)

## Files

Drop finished PNGs into `assets/frugal/` (e.g. `5.png`, `25.png`, `100.png`).
