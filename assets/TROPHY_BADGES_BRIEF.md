# Trophy Badges — Logger, Perfect Month, Categorizer

Match the style of the existing rank/streak badge art already in this app
(medal shape, ribbon, illustrated icon) — same family, your creative call on
the rest.

## Logger — 4 tiers (a ladder, like the streak badges)

Rewards logging transactions over your lifetime, not just a streak. One
badge per tier, escalating in richness the way the streak badges do:

- 100 transactions
- 500 transactions
- 1,000 transactions
- 2,500 transactions (top tier)

## Perfect Month — 1 badge

Earned by logging every single day of one calendar month. A single, one-time
achievement (no ladder).

## Categorizer — 1 badge

Earned by keeping every transaction categorized for 30 days straight. Also a
single, one-time achievement (no ladder).

## Files

Drop finished PNGs into:
- `assets/logger/` (e.g. `100.png`, `500.png`, `1000.png`, `2500.png`)
- `assets/perfect_month/`
- `assets/categorizer/`
